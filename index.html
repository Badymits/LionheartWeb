What is a hero?
Selfless, genuinely good person

Acting voluntarily for the service of others who are in need, whether it is for an individual, a group, or a community. People who don't look for anything in return. People who does it all and does not falter in times of adversity because people look up to them. Showing a brave front can give hope for the people around them.

What makes a hero?
Someone who cares and fights for the greater good

A person who cares for their people and won't tolerate any wickedness or destructive acts towards the community is what some would consider a hero. Showing love and affection and fighting for the greater good for the betterment of the community is what people should strive for.

How do you become a hero?
First, be responsible

It is important to be responsible because this shows your obligation to do things, to make sure that things get done, it shows you have control and being in charge. You have duties to fulfill which could eventually lead to helping another person and possibly brighten their day. Even the little things matter.

Who are the heroes today?
Those who stand up even in the most difficult times

A hero doesn’t have to have special powers, it can be an ordinary every day person. A hero always puts their lives on the line. They risk their own life to safe the lives of others. You step up what I mean by step up is they become brave in extremely brave in very difficult moments. You don’t stand up for what you think is right. You stand up for what you know is right.
